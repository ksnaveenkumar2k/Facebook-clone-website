Benefits of Pages

  • Free and simple: Pages are free. It only takes minutes to create a Page, and we offer several features to manage it.
  • Versatile: Your Page comes with many free tools that help you achieve specific goals based on your business model.
  • Connect with customers: Billions of people use Facebook to communicate with businesses. Use your Page to build loyalty with existing and new customers.
  • Advertise with your Page: Ads can help you grow your brand and audience. You need a Page to advertise on Facebook. You can use Facebook's advertising tools to create ads, set your budget and plan your strategy.

Top features of Pages

  • Business information : Pages show information about your business, such as your address, phone number, hours, categories, unique username and more.
  • Posting: The fastest way to share information on your Page is to post. You can add an action button to encourage people to take specific actions. Use our publishing tools to manage your posting schedule.
  • Messaging : Your Page comes with Inbox, which lets you communicate with people on Facebook, Instagram and Messenger. You can also use tools to organise your messages and automate responses.
  • Insights : Insights can help you find customers who care about what you offer. You can understand actions that people take on your Page, such as how often they like, comment on or share posts. You can also see insights about your competitors' Pages to learn about your industry and audience.
  • Tools : You can use our free Page tools to connect with customers and expand your business. Tools such as Events and Appointments can help you grow your customer base.
